The Life of a Bill

Predicted Philippine Senate bill status from historical legislative data, showing how committee assignment, policy area, filing timing, and author history can signal bill progress.

  • Scraped and prepared bill records from the 15th to 18th Congress.
  • Compared models and selected XGBoost for structured prediction.
  • Explained why most bills remain at First Reading.
Problem Many bills are filed, but few progress. Data Senate records across four Congresses. Method Feature engineering and XGBoost modeling. Result Progress signals became easier to compare. Impact Legislative patterns became explainable.

Statistics R Bootstrap

From Farm to Cup

Used bootstrap confidence intervals to estimate Bukidnon coffee farmers' income more responsibly when the income distribution is uneven and uncertain.

  • Analyzed a synthetic dataset of 200 farmer records.
  • Compared traditional intervals with bootstrap and BCa methods.
  • Translated uncertainty into policy and livelihood context.
Problem Average income hides farmer risk. Data 200 Bukidnon coffee farmer records. Method Bootstrap and BCa confidence intervals. Result Income uncertainty became visible. Impact Better basis for support decisions.
