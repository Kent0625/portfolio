# Credibility Polish Design

## Goal

Make the portfolio feel more direct, credible, and aligned with the resume without changing the static HTML/CSS stack.

## Approved Direction

Use a focused homepage polish:

- Clean hero wording and replace the rotating focus phrase with one calm static line.
- Strengthen the interactive preview panels so they feel more like a mini data workflow.
- Add Inventory Decision Support System as a business analytics/dashboard project to align the site with the resume.
- Improve contact copy so it speaks directly to datasets, dashboards, internships, and project opportunities.
- Add a concise "Available for" list in the contact area.

## Content Choices

### Hero

Keep Kent's name as the headline. Update the body copy to:

"I build explainable data science projects that turn messy datasets into clean insights, machine learning models, dashboards, and decision-ready stories."

Replace the animated focus rail with:

"Focused on Data Science, Machine Learning, Dashboards, and AI-powered analytics."

### Projects

Recommended order:

1. The Life of a Bill
2. Inventory Decision Support System
3. From Farm to Cup
4. E-Eye Screening Concept
5. SmartTripCDO

Inventory DSS will be shown as a practical dashboard/business analytics project with a mailto action until a repo or screenshot is available.

### Interactive Preview

Keep the no-JavaScript radio tabs, but make panel content more concrete:

- EDA: data quality checks, distributions, and segment comparison.
- Model: model comparison, validation, and explainability.
- Dashboard: KPI cards, trend panels, and status summaries.
- Insights: recommendation, limitation, and next action.

### Contact

Update the footer headline to:

"Have a dataset, dashboard idea, or internship opportunity? Let's turn it into a clear data project."

Add an "Available for" list:

- Data Science Internship
- Analytics Internship
- ML Internship
- Dashboard / Reporting Projects

## Constraints

- Keep the site static.
- Do not add a contact form backend.
- Do not add fake links or fake assets.
- Preserve the resume, GitHub, LinkedIn, and email actions.
- Verify locally, push to GitHub, and confirm Render updates.
