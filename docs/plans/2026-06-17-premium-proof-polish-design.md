# Premium Proof Polish Design

## Goal
Make the portfolio feel more premium and visually proven while keeping the current clean one-page structure.

## Approved Direction
Apply a premium proof polish pass:
- Add compact project fact badges.
- Strengthen Inventory DSS and E-Eye proof visuals.
- Make the primary call to action more dominant.
- Add more spacing between dense cards and sections.
- Move the color suite from olive-green to a deeper data-science navy with cyan and emerald accents.

## Design Notes
The palette should feel technical and recruiter-friendly, not decorative. Use navy surfaces for depth, cyan for primary action and links, emerald for analytics/proof accents, and amber only for small warnings or highlight details.

## Non-goals
- Do not change the overall site architecture.
- Do not add JavaScript or dependencies.
- Do not invent screenshots or fake deployments.
- Do not remove existing poster/project links.

## Verification
- Confirm project facts appear for all five projects.
- Confirm Inventory DSS and E-Eye proof visuals still render.
- Confirm desktop and mobile have no horizontal overflow.
- Confirm Render serves the final update.
